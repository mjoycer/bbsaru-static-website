WHO WE ARE

BB SaRu happened because of the two people who have a lot of interest in samgyupsal and who’s favorite place to eat is Korean Samgyupsal.

BB SaRu was established in Taguig, Philippines in the year 2021, and is still serving up delicious and affordable meals today.

The food business is named after Samuel and Rue (SaRu) and BB is Bento Box.

MISSION

To bring people food that is safe and of high quality. In addition to health and nutrition, BB SaRu bring consumers the vital ingredients of taste and pleasure.

VISION

To satisfy people’s cravings without paying too much.
