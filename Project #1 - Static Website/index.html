WHAT'S BB SARU?

BB SaRu is meal placed in a Bento Box. Each Bento Box is a complete meal composed of rice, lettuce, braised potatoes, egg roll, kimchi, cheese and other sauces and a choice of meat between pork and beef. We also released a new yakiniku flavor that can also be made spicy.
